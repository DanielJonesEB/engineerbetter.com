Would you like to work with us?

What do we offer?

  • A team built on trust and transparency, where what you believe is more important than what you know.
  • Interesting and rewarding work, with customers ranging from global brands to software startups.
  • A chance to make people better at making computers better.

Who are we looking for?

We're currently looking to have conversations with contractors, or people interested in becoming contractors.

We’re looking for people who are happy to integrate into customer teams, working on site mainly in the London area, usually on a 3-6 month basis.

Above all else, we’d like to find people who share our values.

In terms of skills and background, we’re looking for the following:

  • Experience with Cloud Foundry, BOSH and the surrounding technology ecosystem
  • Experience of pairing and working in agile teams
  • TDD, especially in Java, Go or Ruby
  • Familiarity with continuous delivery

If working with EngineerBetter sounds like something you'd be interested in, then please let us know.
