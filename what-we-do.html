Enablement Through Delivery

We enable customers to deliver business value faster with Cloud Foundry by pair-programming with their engineers over a number of months. We work from an ordered backlog, continuously delivering usable solutions into production.

The approach of pair-programming is the most successful method for increasing knowledge in our customers' businesses whilst also delivering production systems.

Examples of the solutions we've delivered include:

  • Concourse continuous delivery pipelines to stand up entire Cloud Foundry environments
  • Cloud Foundry buildpacks
  • Service brokers, BOSH releases and Pivotal Cloud Foundry tiles
  • Utilities to make operating Cloud Foundry easier

Training

We provide on-site hands-on, instructor-led Cloud Foundry training. After running training at Cloud Foundry Summit the Cloud Foundry Foundation asked us to build the official training course for the Cloud Foundry Certified Developer exam.

We offer two levels of course. The aim of both of them is to establish a robust and accurate mental model of Cloud Foundry, by experiencing how to use the platform through practical exercises. Students are not given step-by-step instructions; instead the practical exercises lead them to discover solutions through use of the CF CLI and the CF documentation.

Find out more about our training courses:

  • Zero to Hero
  • Cloud Foundry Certified Developer

Consultancy

We also offer advice and guidance on the use of Cloud Foundry, BOSH and Concourse, continuous delivery of cloud-native microservices, and the organisational change required to deliver business value faster.

Product Development

We'll help you build your Cloud Foundry related product and get it into the hands of users as quickly as possible. This can be can in the form of enablement through delivery if you're looking to acquire skills, or we can provide a team who will work with your stakeholders and customers to build the software through from inception to production. We can help with product management, test driven development and engineering values, so your product team can embrace change over time.
