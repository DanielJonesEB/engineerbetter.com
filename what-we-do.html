What We Do

We offer holistic services to help you deliver value faster with Cloud Foundry.

Our work

We help organisations use Cloud Foundry, BOSH and Concourse by offering enablement through delivery, consultancy and training. Our customers include some of the world's biggest banks, global wealth management enterprises, FTSE 100 retailers, non-profit organisations and software vendors.

We offer:

  • Enablement through delivery
  • Consultancy
  • Training

Enablement Through Delivery

We enable customers to deliver business value faster with Cloud Foundry by pair-programming with their engineers over a number of months. We work from an ordered backlog, continuously delivering usable solutions into production.

The approach of pair-programming is the most successful method for increasing knowledge in our customers' businesses whilst also delivering production systems.

Examples of the solutions we've delivered include:

  • Concourse continuous delivery pipelines to stand up entire Cloud Foundry environments
  • Cloud Foundry buildpacks
  • Service brokers, BOSH releases and Pivotal Cloud Foundry tiles
  • Utilities to make operating Cloud Foundry easier

Consultancy

We also offer advice and guidance on the use of Cloud Foundry, BOSH and Concourse, continuous delivery of cloud-native microservices, and the organisational change required to deliver business value faster.

Training

We provide on-site hands-on, instructor-led Cloud Foundry training. In fact, the Cloud Foundry Foundation asked us to provide training at Cloud Foundry Summit Frankfurt.
